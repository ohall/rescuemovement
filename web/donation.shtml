Make a Donation

There are many different ways to show that you believe in what Rescue Movement is committed to achieve. Are you an equestrian destined to become an Ambassador? Is your company proud to sponsor the good intentions of our non-profit? Or are you simply someone who cares about giving equine's a second chance?

Whatever the reason, it is important for you to know that you make a difference with your contribution to Rescue Movement. Almost $ .95 of every dollar contributed goes directly to our program initiatives. Be an Ambassador -- Ride to Raise Awareness and Support our Rescue Mission. Be a Sponsor -- Donate to the rescue and rehabilitation of horses and ponies who are facing slaughter. Our first goal is to purchase a barn with an indoor and outdoor ring and at least 8-10 stalls to support our rescue operation.

US-based donations are tax deductible, and receipts will be issued following your donation.

We accept online donations via PayPal!
