Our Mission

To provide life and hope in the equine community and in doing so, touch the lives of many people along the way. We believe, with humane companionship and financial help, that every incremental effort given for “charity” should be given to the charity. Putting this philosophy into action, the National Equine Rescue Foundation Inc. was established in 2012.

Rescue Movement is a division of National Equine Rescue Foundation Inc., a certified 501(c)(3) charity creating a presence in the equine show world; an organization dedicated to the rescue of horses and ponies facing slaughter and/or abandonment.

We are trying to raise awareness and funding for our organization through:

  • • Our Rider Ambassadors program, where riders purchase and wear our colors in the show ring. Ambassadors give 2% of show earnings to our mission and they are spokespersons for our cause. Money is given to multiple hand-picked and researched rescues around the USA.
  • • Sponsors who see the value and integrity of our mission and wish to be associated with Rescue Movement. Donations of product, financial help and volunteers allow the reinforcement of our philosophy.
  • • Grant funding that will provide Rescue Movement with the marketing tools needed to position us as a viable partner to the equine community, working toward social change.
  • • Fundraising and actively participating in the show circuit (from NY to Florida) encouraging sponsors with enhanced visibility and extended relationships within the equine community.
