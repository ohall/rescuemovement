Meet Fyra

"The truth is you don't know what is going to happen tomorrow. Life is a crazy ride, and nothing is guaranteed."
- Eminem

Our greatest rescue yet is beautiful "Fyra." It took lots of patience, time and belief in her to be able to even approach Fyra as she was abused and left out in a paddock, where she was barely fed, and even when she was 3 months pregnant you could see her ribs. Today, the untouchable mare stands to be groomed, shod, vets, and has been learning how to be ridden and has now been backed two times. She is now on vacation until a healthy baby arrives! She will make her debut most likely in the hunter ring.
