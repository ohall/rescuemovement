Ambassadors

To be an Ambassador means you wish to represent Rescue Movement by wearing the colors of Rescue Movement in the show ring. When you see the black white & gold, know the rider has heart and is proud to be a part of what Rescue Movement stands for. In addition, you will be acting as a spokesperson to raise awareness for Rescue Movement among your friends, local community, at horse shows and in the media.

To be considered as an Ambassador, please fill out the form below and click Submit.
